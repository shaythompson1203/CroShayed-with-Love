Handcrafted with Passion and Purpose

CroShayed with Love is built on passion, creativity, and a deep appreciation for handcrafted work. Shay creates customized crocheted items with care and attention to detail, ensuring each piece is as unique as the person receiving it. Her journey began at a young age when her father first introduced her to crocheting, and over the years, she has continued to refine her skills through dedication, practice, and self-teaching. What started as a meaningful hobby has grown into a heartfelt craft that reflects both tradition and personal expression.

Creating Meaningful, One-of-a-Kind Pieces

Through CroShayed with Love, Shay is excited to share her creations beyond family and friends and connect with a wider community. Each item she makes is thoughtfully designed and crafted to bring comfort, beauty, and joy. Crocheting is more than just a skill to Shay—it is a creative outlet that allows her to turn ideas into tangible, one-of-a-kind pieces. She takes pride in every stitch and considers it an honor to create items that hold special meaning for others.
